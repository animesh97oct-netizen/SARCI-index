# SARCI — South & Central Asia Risk Composite Index

> Built to answer one question: which countries in South & Central Asia 
> will destabilise first when the next geopolitical shock hits?

A novel 4-pillar quantitative geopolitical risk composite index covering 
13 countries in South and Central Asia.

**Live Dashboard:** https://animesh97oct-netizen.github.io/SARCI-index

**Built from:** IMF WEO April 2026 · ACLED Dec 2024 · UN Comtrade · World Bank WGI 2024
